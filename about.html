The origin story
It Started as a Bracket

For years, Leah had one dream: a definitive New England ice cream bracket. Every shop, head-to-head, until one supreme champion emerged. The problem? A bracket means going back — and when there are this many new places to try, who has time to go back?

"We just want to try new ones."

So instead of a bracket, we built this — a living, breathing map of every ice cream spot Leah has ever rated, reviewed, and strong-opinioned about. Ice cream isn't just her favorite food. It's a daily ritual, a travel priority, and an art form she takes very seriously.

From homemade scoops in hidden Cambridge shops to soft serve on the side of the road, if it's frozen and creamy, Leah has thoughts. Many thoughts. With a scoring system to back them up.
